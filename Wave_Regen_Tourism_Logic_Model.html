  • Natural capital is protected and enhanced as a foundational asset in tourism and coastal development decision-making
  • Contribution to national economic diversification through high-value, nature-based tourism
  • Saudi Arabia is positioned as a global leader in regenerative tourism, catalyzing private investment into nature-positive infrastructure and business models
